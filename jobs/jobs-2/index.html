Marketing and sales assistant

Marketing and sales support to the co-founder of qatent(INRIA technology incubated at Agoranov)

Context

qatent, a legaltech that has raised its first round of financing, is looking for a sales assistant. The aim is to assist the co-founder on marketing and sales activities.

Emerged from and supported by INRIA (the French National Computer Science Research Center), the company develops an AI platform to help drafting patent applications. You will work in a team of 8 people of 5 nationalities within the AGORANOV incubator in the 6th district of Paris (where success stories such as Doctolib, Dataiku, Ynsect, Criteo were created).

After more than one year of IT development, we have collected the contact information of many prospects. In the first phase, you will learn about our business and our positioning, then you will co-construct your sales tools and apply them, until the closing of sales.

Responsibilities

Under the direct supervision of one of the two co-founders, you will first work on the marketing of the qatent offer, and, once the positioning has been assimilated and the website is ready, you will support the CEO and co-founder with the commercial activity.

Marketing (1st part of the internship, supported by an experienced consultant)
  • copywriting, you will deploy the marketing strategy on the website contents (help pages, FAQ, datasheets, ...)
  • screencast realization, from scripts to online publishing and tracking
Sales (2nd part of the internship)

You will be in charge of the existing prospects (UK, DE, USA):

  • co-construction of the commercial framework;
  • telemarketing;
  • debriefing and continuous improvement of the sales pitch;
  • demos of the service (client site or videoconference);
  • customer follow-up until closing.

The objective of the internship is therefore concrete and will have a real impact on the young company.

Your profile

  • Business/marketing background
  • At ease in English
  • With a first commercial experience (in internship, association or summer job)
  • Proven writing skills
  • Understanding of patents is a plus, at least interest

The duration of the internship is negotiable (from 3 to 6 months).
Minimum 800 euros per month, fixed-term or permanent contract possible afterwards. Partly remote work possible.
Send your CV and an example of the writing you have done to François Veltz: fv@qatent.com
